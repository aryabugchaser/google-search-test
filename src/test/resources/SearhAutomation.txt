Scenario: Search with Valid Query
Steps:
1. Open Google homepage
2. Enter "Selenium WebDriver" in the search bar
3. Click the search button
Expected Result:
Search results for "Selenium WebDriver" are displayed

Scenario: Search with Special Characters
Steps:
1. Open Google homepage
2. Enter "@%*!@#" in the search bar
3. Click the search button
Expected Result:
Search results are displayed and no error occurs

Scenario: Empty Search
Steps:
1. Open Google homepage
2. Leave the search bar empty
3. Click the search button
Expected Result:
No results page appears or user is prompted to enter a search term
